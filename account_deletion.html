Account & Data Deletion

EasyInfoView allows users to delete their account and all related data directly within the app. Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to request account deletion

  1. Open the EasyInfoView app and go to Admin → Info → Request deletion.
  2. Your account will be marked for deletion and a 7-day waiting period begins.
  3. After 7 days, you can return to the same page and tap Confirm deletion.
  4. All your displays, items, and related data will be permanently removed from our systems.
  5. The 7 day period exists to reduce risk for accidental data deletions.

Need help?

If you can no longer access your account, please contact our support team and include your registered email address. We will process the deletion manually.

Email: support@easyinfoview.com

Data retention

After deletion, an anonymized audit log may remain for security or legal compliance purposes, but it contains no personal information and cannot be used to identify you.
