Privacy Policy for EasyInfoView

Last updated: October 2025

1. Overview

EasyInfoView is a display and content management application that allows users to create, edit, and view informational displays (“Displays”) and items (“Items”) across their own devices. This app is developed and maintained by an independent developer.

2. Data We Collect

EasyInfoView collects and stores only the minimum data necessary to provide the service. This includes:

  • User authentication data (email address and password) handled securely by Supabase Auth.
  • Display and Item content created by the user (such as names, prices, or text descriptions).
  • Basic technical metadata such as timestamps and device identifiers used only for synchronization and security.

No sensitive personal data (such as health, biometric, or financial information) is collected or processed.

3. Camera Usage

The app may request permission to use the device’s camera solely for scanning QR codes when the user chooses to transfer or enter an access key between devices. The camera feed is used only for reading QR codes — no images or videos are captured, stored, or transmitted.

4. Data Storage and Security

User data is securely stored in the Supabase backend, located within the European Union (or a region selected by Supabase). All connections between the app and server are encrypted using HTTPS.

5. Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their accounts and all associated data from within the app at any time. Deleted accounts are permanently removed after a short grace period, except for minimal anonymized audit logs retained for legal or technical integrity purposes.

6. Third-Party Services

EasyInfoView uses Supabase (https://supabase.com) for authentication, database, and storage services. These services operate in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

7. Children’s Privacy

This application is intended for general professional and business use. It is not specifically directed to children under 13 years of age, and no personal data is knowingly collected from minors.
